Welcome to Monster Survivors! Test your survival skills in a thrilling adventure game online. Can you survive the monsters?

Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an action-packed game where you have to fight against waves of monsters. Build your survival strategies, upgrade your gear, and survive as long as you can! Play now and enjoy the challenge!

How to Play

Use your skills to survive the monster attacks. Collect resources, upgrade your equipment, and explore different levels as the challenge increases.
